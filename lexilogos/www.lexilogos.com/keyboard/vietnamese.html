Instructions

To type directly with the computer keyboard:

  • Type dd > đ
  • Type aa > ă | oo > ơ | uu > ư
  • Type aaa > â | ee > ê | ooo > ô
  • Type a number for the tone: a2, a3, a4, a5, a6 > à, á, ạ, ã, ả
    or type = to change the tone: a=, a==, a===, a=====

Copy [Ctrl]+[C] & Paste [Ctrl]+[V]

The Vietnamese tones

1 ngang no mark
2 huyền grave accent
3 sắc acute accent
4 nặng dot below
5 ngã tilde above
6 hỏi hook above

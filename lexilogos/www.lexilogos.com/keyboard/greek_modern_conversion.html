Instructions

- The conversion in Modern Greek is according to Modern Greek pronunciation:
η, ι, υ, οι, ει, υι are transcribed i
ο, ω are transcribed o

- The transliteration converts each letter of the Greek alphabet in a letter of the Latin alphabet for the Ancient Greek:
η, ι, υ, ω are transcribed ē, i, u, ō

Instructions

Click on a consonant then a vowel to form a syllable.

Only the most common characters are displayed; however, all characters can be typed using the computer keyboard.


To type directly with the computer keyboard:

1) Type Latin characters with a space key between each syllable to convert the Latin characters in a Korean character.

2) Type a space key twice between two words.

example: type han guk mal to get: 한국말


Type g or k, d or t, b or p for ㄱ, ㄷ, ㅂ
and the capitals K, T, P for ㅋ, ㅌ, ㅍ

The characters ㄱ, ㄷ, ㅂ are usually transcribed g, d, b before a vowel and k, t, p before a consonant or at the end of the word.
